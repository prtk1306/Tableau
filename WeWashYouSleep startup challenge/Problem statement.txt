WeWashYouSleep startup Challenge:

You are a Data Scientist for a laundry-pickup services startup WeWashYouSleep. This is a relatively small company and they can not compete with the big players in the major cities. The company's strategy is to build a vast network in smaller cities.

WeWashYouSleep already had a strong presence in 140 locations and recently opened stores in 10 new cities. Additionally, the company has two separate sales regions.

You have been assigned two analytics tasks:

1) Identify which of the two sales regions in performing better (outperforms the other in 2 of the 3 metrics):
    a. AVG Revenue per city
    b. AVG Marketing spend per city (less is better)
    c. AVG ROMI per city (Revenue/marketing spend)
2) Identify which of the 10 new locations have the best potential for the company to invest more funds into marketing.

Some of the amazing tasks performed while executing this exercise:
* Created custom territories via Groups
* Created custom territories via Geographic Roles
* Use highlighters in Tableau
* Performed Cluster Analysis in Tableau
* Use domain knowledge to add external datasets into our analysis
* Created cross-database joins
* Combined Regression Modeling with Clustering
* Save clusters for further analytics
